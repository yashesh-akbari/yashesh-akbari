This Privacy Policy outlines the handling of personal information collected from individuals participating in blood donation activities in India. It adheres to the regulations and guidelines set forth by Indian laws regarding data protection and privacy.

  1. Collection of Personal Information:
  • Personal information may be collected from blood donors during registration, health screenings, and donation processes.
  • Information collected may include name, contact details, government-issued identification, medical history, and demographic data.
  1. Purpose of Data Collection:
  • Personal information is collected solely for the purpose of facilitating blood donation activities, ensuring donor eligibility, and maintaining blood supply safety.
  • Information may be used to communicate with donors, schedule donation appointments, and provide post-donation care instructions.
  1. Use of Personal Information:
  • Personal information collected from donors will be used only for the intended purpose stated at the time of collection.
  • Information may be used for internal record-keeping, statistical analysis, and reporting as required by regulatory authorities.
  1. Confidentiality and Security:
  • Donor information will be treated with strict confidentiality and safeguarded against unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • Appropriate security measures will be implemented to protect personal data from loss, misuse, or alteration.
  1. Disclosure of Information:
  • Personal information will not be disclosed to third parties without explicit consent, except as required by law or regulatory authorities.
  • Information may be shared with healthcare professionals, blood banks, or government agencies involved in blood donation and public health initiatives.
  1. Retention of Data:
  • Personal information collected from donors will be retained only for the duration necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Data may be retained for a longer period if required by law or for archival and research purposes.
  1. Rights of Donors:
  • Donors have the right to access, update, or correct their personal information held by blood banks or donation centers.
  • Donors may request the deletion of their data or opt-out of further communication or donation-related activities.
  1. Consent:
  • By participating in blood donation activities, donors consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of their personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy.
  • Donors have the right to withdraw consent at any time, subject to legal or contractual obligations.
  1. Updates to Privacy Policy:
  • This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in laws, regulations, or organizational practices.
  • Updated versions of the Privacy Policy will be made available to donors through appropriate channels.

By engaging in blood donation activities, donors acknowledge their understanding of this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of their personal information in accordance with Indian regulations and standards.
